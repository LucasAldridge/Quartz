## Color in architeture
___
- Rawness of nature
	- Color that is a pure byproduct of material used
		- Great mosque of Mali, like it rose from the ground
- Site and color
	- Integral part of the landscape. 
		- Wooden house in Costa rica isnt it's color because it's wood, it's particularly the LOCAL wood
		- The cobblestones of a castle made of stones found NEARBY
- Process of nature
	- If there is an orange in the stone nearby a place, their materials are sure to maintain some of that orange found nearby
	- It's a derivative of the ground and resources it sits on
- Symbolic uses of color
	- In Sweden, a "REAL" house needs to be red
	- The color is a BYPRODUCT of the culture
	- Norway museum have a specific color per room based on the kind of art in there.
	- Dijon in France has polychromatic roofs.
