Economics definition: The study of the allocation and use of scarce resources to satisfy unlimited human wants.
Trade-off: Something you have to give up to get something else
Opportunity cost: The highest valued alternative you have to give up to get something else... the GREATEST tradeoff

As you spend your time and money making one thing, making another is less feasible. There is an attainable and unattainable amount or production.
