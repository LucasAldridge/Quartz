Nobody can make a good bag. This is a problem for me, as I need a bag, and a good one at that. I like the vibe of a zip-up duffel bag, but the laptop-support for a large backpack. However, nobody makes bags like this. If you think they do, multiple hours of research proves you wrong. 

This has lead me to the conclusion that making my own bag is the easiest option for me. This will be a design document for that bag, and as I build I will add notes, suggestions, etc, in case you wanna make your own bag, or just understand how I went about making mine. 

# Goals
Create a bag that combines the wide-opening and cylinder shape of a duffel bag with the laptop-compatibility of a backpack.  I want it made out of canvas, wearable like a backpack, and easily held like a duffel bag. 

# Current plan 
The plan right now is a half circle of a bag that's flat on the bottom. There will be a zipper going around half of the bottom rectangle creating the bag's flat bottom, starting at the midpoint of one long side, and ending at the midpoint of the other long side. Padding on the bottom of the bag is a maybe. Coming up from this flat rectangle i the rest of the half cylinder. This is essentially the duffel bag portion. A zipper will run longways on  top of the half cylinder. Then, two side bags will also run lengthwise on the side of the main cylinder. These will also have zippers running longways on the top.
