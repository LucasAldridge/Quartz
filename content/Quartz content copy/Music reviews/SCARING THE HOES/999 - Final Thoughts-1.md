Tom introduced me to this album, and I had never really given it a full listen until I took a flight from London to Mykonos. I took notes on my phone as I listened... and then transferred them here in my free time. 

Scaring the Hoes is a fantastic album. I'm pretty sure my ratings average out to something like an 8.5, but I'm gonna round up as even some of the "low points" were due to them being worse only by contrast to the rest of the album. The only actual skip is Jack Harlow Combo Meal. 

Overall album rating: 9/10
