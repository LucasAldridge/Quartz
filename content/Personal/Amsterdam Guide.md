This is hopefully a solid guide to Amsterdam. I stayed here a few days so I have a bit to say that's hopefully relevant.

# Where to stay

There are many hotels but my recommendation is that you stay where I did, in Overtoom. 

1) The Iron Horse
	- Cheap
	- Pretty nice
	- Private bathrooms
	- Rooms can accommodate like 4 or more people
2) Huygen's place 
	- Cheap
	- Feels kinda luxury
	- Private bathroom
	- Beautiful courtyard where you're allowed to smoke (if you wanna try the weed)
# Food

Lets get it straight... go to Foodhallen. It's kinda like Optimist Hall except its legal for you to drink bier and it's in Amsterdam. It's a 15 minute walk from those hotels I mentioned. 

## Other food places
1) Dominos
	- Goated spot. Right by both of those hotels
2) Joe and The Juice
	- They got breakfast. Tastes great!
3) any of the little bar/tapas places around
	- There's a lot of these. Grab a snack and a Heineken. It's worth

# Weed

I'm not a weed guy, but I did try it while I was there. Strangely, their top shelf stuff is from California. Most standard stuff is about 10 Euro for a gram. Cali weed, however, is around 25 Euro a gram. This kinda leads me to the idea that if you want to just smoke weed... go to California. It's probably cheaper, you can go to the beach, and there's less that can go wrong with travel.
